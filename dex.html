this website

why did i make this website?

I’ve had a spark of interest in code in me for a while until I took VS.net in my sophomore year and swore never to code again. But then I hit a low point at the end of my junior year and found it again by suddenly seeing videos of people enjoying building their games and websites on Instagram reels. The interest was suddenly rekindled when my dad gave me a web development bootcamp on Udemy.

This website is for me to learn something new not just about HTML, CSS, and JavaScript but also about myself as I compiled my personality with what I could do at the time. It is also a stepping stone for me to hopefully work on other projects important to me in the near future! Thank you for taking the time to go through my first ever website I hope this will leave a meaningful digital footprint when I look back.
